Top Study Tips

01

Active Recall

Instead of passively re-reading notes, test yourself regularly. This strengthens memory retention and identifies knowledge gaps effectively.

02

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-30 minute break. This maintains focus and prevents burnout.

03

Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 1 week, etc.). This leverages the spacing effect for long-term retention.

04

Feynman Technique

Explain concepts in simple terms as if teaching a child. This reveals true understanding and highlights areas needing more study.

05

Practice Testing

Regularly take practice tests under exam conditions. This builds exam stamina and improves performance under pressure.
